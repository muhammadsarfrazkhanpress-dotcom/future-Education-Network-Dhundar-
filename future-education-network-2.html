Who We Are

A Welfare Network Serving Through Education

Future Education Network Dhundar is a non-profit, voluntary welfare organization working at the grassroots level to promote education in the Dhundar region of Azad Kashmir, Pakistan.

We are not an institution — we are a movement. We collaborate with existing schools, colleges, and government bodies to bridge gaps in education, connect deserving students with scholarships, and raise awareness through impactful events.

Our organization is built on the principles of integrity, transparency, and mutual cooperation.
